HOME REMEDY

Excessive Humidity & Poor Airflow

Immediate: Prune infected lower leaves and dispose of them far from the garden site.

Environmental: Switch to drip irrigation to keep foliage dry; increase spacing between plants.

Prevention: Apply a 1:9 milk-to-water solution spray as a mild natural antiseptic for surrounding healthy plants.

Care Notes for Potato

Hilling

Mound soil around stems to prevent tubers from turning green and bitter due to light exposure.

Harvest Timing

Wait until the foliage dies back completely. This allows the skins to thicken for better storage.

Storage

Keep in a dark, cool, and well-ventilated place. Never store potatoes with onions or apples.
